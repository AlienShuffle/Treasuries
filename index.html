TIPS Ladder Builder — Help

All calculations run in your browser. Prices updated daily ~1 PM ET from FedInvest.

Rebalance — for existing ladder holders rebalancing into newly issued TIPS. Assumes you want to keep using the same lower bracket TIPS you originally purchased.
Build — designs a new ladder from scratch with zero existing holdings.

Key Concepts

DARA — Desired Annual Real Amount. Your target annual income in today's (inflation-adjusted) dollars. Each rung is sized to produce this amount.

Gap Years — Years with no issued TIPS (currently 2037–2039). The ladder holds excess bonds in bracket years flanking the gap to match the average duration of the missing maturities.

Bracket Years — Upper bracket: always the Feb 2040 TIPS. Lower bracket: for Build, the latest issued 10-year TIPS before the gap; for Rebalance, the TIPS before the gap with the largest existing holdings. Excess bonds in these years duration-match the gap.

RefCPI — Reference CPI value used for all inflation adjustments.

Mode: Rebalance

For existing TIPS ladder holders. Sells excess bracket bonds and buys into newly available maturities (former gap years). Assumes you want to continue using your original lower bracket TIPS.

Holdings CSV — Two columns: cusip and qty. A sample is pre-filled when you click the file input.

DARA — Leave blank to auto-infer from holdings.

Method — Gap: rebalances only the bracket years and previous gap years (sells excess from brackets, buys into previous gap years). Full: rebalances all rungs across the full contiguous ladder range.

Output (Simple tab) — CUSIP · Maturity · FY · Qty · Target · Delta · Cash Δ. No-change rows dimmed; bracket rows blue.
Output (Detail tab) — Full computation with ARA before/after, later maturity interest, weights.

Net Cash — Net proceeds (sells minus buys). Negative = net purchase.

Mode: Build

Designs a new TIPS ladder from scratch with zero existing holdings. Currently uses the latest-maturing TIPS in each funded year.

DARA (required) — Enter any amount; up/down arrows adjust in $1,000 increments for convenience. Last Year — Final funded year; first year is always the current calendar year.

Output columns:
FY Qty — bonds for the rung · Excess Qty — extra bonds in bracket years · Total Qty — sum
FY Amount ≈ DARA · FY Cost — cost of FY bonds
Excess Amount / Excess Cost — bracket rows only

Total Cost — full ladder purchase cost.

Duration Matching

Synthetic TIPS are created for gap years using yield interpolation between the bracket anchors. Excess bonds are split between the lower and upper bracket so their weighted duration matches the average duration of the gap years:

lowerWeight × lowerDuration + upperWeight × upperDuration = gapAvgDuration
e.g.  0.46 × 9.1 (2036) + 0.54 × 12.1 (2040) = 10.7
